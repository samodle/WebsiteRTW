About Us

Recycle the World Inc. is a nonprofit corporation organized and operated exclusively for charitable purposes, specifically to research new technologies to recycle all components of the waste stream, research conversion technologies that decrease or eliminate all need for landfills and create feedstocks for industry, and educate municipalities so that they can reduce or eliminate the need to send waste to landfills.

Research

We will conduct research into recycling science and technology benefiting the environment and the health of our citizens.

Eliminate Landfills

We feel we can positively affect the environmental conditions that are associated with municipal solid waste. Landfills are environmental time bombs.

Education

We will educate municipalities on how to become landfill free and recycle all waste beneficially.

Research

We will research alternative products and technologies that will enhance the value of the products that can be manufactured from the elements of the waste stream. The concentration of this research will be to develop new products that can be manufactured from each category of recoverable resource that will reduce the need for continual depletion of natural resources.

Eliminate Landfills

Landfills negatively affect the groundwater and cause greenhouse gas emissions. We can reduce or eliminate the landfills with specific research and education. This will benefit both the citizens of today and future generations. .
